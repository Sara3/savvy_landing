Plaid & Financial Data

To connect your bank accounts, Savvy uses Plaid Technologies, Inc. Plaid is a trusted financial data network used by Venmo, Robinhood, Coinbase, and thousands of other apps.

Read-only access only. Savvy has no ability to move, transfer, or withdraw funds from your accounts. We access transaction history and balances to power Savvy's features ‚Äî nothing else.

When you connect a financial account through Plaid:

  • You authenticate directly with your bank ‚Äî Savvy never sees your banking username or password.
  • Plaid transmits your financial data to us using bank-level encryption.
  • Your data is governed by both this Privacy Policy and Plaid's End User Privacy Policy.
  • You can revoke Plaid access at any time ‚Äî in Savvy under Settings ‚Üí Connected Accounts, or directly at my.plaid.com.

Data Retention

We retain your personal information for as long as your account is active or as needed to provide the service. Specifically:

  • Account data is retained for the duration of your account and deleted within 30 days of account closure.

You may request deletion of your account and associated data at any time by emailing privacy@savvyfinance.io. We will process deletion requests within 30 days, subject to any legal retention requirements.
